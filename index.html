Did you know? The Maillard reaction is responsible for the delicious crust on a perfectly grilled steak. This chemical reaction occurs between amino acids and reducing sugars when exposed to high heat, creating complex flavors and a rich brown color. It's the same reaction that gives toasted bread and roasted coffee their distinct taste!

FAQ

What is a steakhouse?

A steakhouse is a restaurant specialized in preparing and serving beef steaks, cooked in various styles.

What are the most popular types of beef steak?

The most popular types are Ribeye, T-Bone, Filet Mignon, New York Strip, and Porterhouse.

How should a steak be cooked properly?

A good steak should be cooked at high temperatures to form a caramelized crust, while the inside remains juicy. The doneness levels include rare, medium-rare, medium, medium-well, and well-done.

What are the best side dishes for a steak?

Some of the most popular side dishes include French fries, mashed potatoes, grilled vegetables, salads, and butter or pepper-based sauces.

What is the most tender cut of beef?

Filet Mignon is considered the most tender cut of beef because it comes from a part of the muscle that is less used.
